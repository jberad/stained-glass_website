Jessica is a stained glass artist based in Pittsburgh, creating custom works of art glass and restoring old or broken stained glass.

Restoration

Lead and solder joints within a stained glass window get brittle and/or oxidize with age causing deterioration and broken glass. In addition, daily fluctuations in atmospheric temperature cause stained glass to expand and contract. Repeated expansion and contraction can cause stained glass to bow. Jess is experienced in the repair, re-leading, preservation, and conservation of older stained glass.
